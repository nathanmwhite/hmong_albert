vim yawg..(leej txiv npe) nws leej tub ces nws txha hlob duav nws cev,
nws thiaj loj teg saws tau sev.
Yog tias tseem yuav mus xwb no mas peb thiaj hais cov lus no kuj hais
tsawg tsawg thiab xwb, nws hais li nram no.
Tuav tus neeg lub npe ces, thov koj os mog hnub no "tuav tus txiv tsev
lub npe" peb cov kwv tij txiv tub peb tsa peb ncej nthab peb muab peb
tub nyab, peb tsa peb ncej tses peb muab peb tub sev, peb tsis pom qab
muab zog peb thov koj lub dag lub zog, plhu txiaj plhu ntsig coj mus
pab laj naim laj txis mus ntaiv hnub ntaiv hli los koj ua siab ntev
nawb mog, no ces cia li pe xwb. Qhov thov dag zog thiab qhov uas ua
tshoob tiav tag los tsev kuj ua tsaug ib nyuag txawv me ntsis thiab.
Koj kuj tau tso qav tso nug cia, tso qoob tso loo tseg.  Koj kuj tau tuaj pab kuv niam kuv
txiv peb mus tswj ncauj xaiv ncauj lug,
Qhov no los peb tsis hais lawm qhov uas hais tias tso qav tso num cia
tso qoob tso loo tseg, no peb hais rau fab kev ploj qaug. Yog tshoob
kos lawm peb tsis muab cov lus hais tias, ncauj xaiv ncauj lug, coj
los hais txuam rau hauv, cov lus no yog lus plaub lus ntug lawm.
