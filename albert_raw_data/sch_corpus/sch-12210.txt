Sijhawm no muaj ib daig nploojtsawb qhua yuj yawvyeev tuaj hauv peb
lub zos no. Sawvdaws yuav tau xyuamxim ov, yog nej muaj sijhawm no,
mus saib cov neejtsa chawqis nawb. Lawv daim tebtsawb ces nws twb rho
txuas ntov du lug lawm laiv
Nej txhob quav ntsej nrog nws txuas lus, vim yog nej teb nws es nws
tau ko thaum twg ces, nws yuav luaj zaj luaj zus nto peb daim me
tebnpleg no thiab. Kuv yeej paub tias muaj tus puav phoojywg Hmoob
nyiam-m nrog nws tham. Tabsis zag nuav tsis zoo li zag u lawm.
