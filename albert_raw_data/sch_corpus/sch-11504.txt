lus suav lawm.

lus.

li, tsi meej lawm, nws yog hu ua "txiv muaj kua" no tab sis muab hu
sis lawm, puav leej yog lus Hmoob.

li Hmoob hu.

ntau hom suav ib pab hais txawv me ntsis thiab.
