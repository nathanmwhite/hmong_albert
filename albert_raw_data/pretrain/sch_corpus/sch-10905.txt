Yog lawm, yuav tau saib tuab quas zoo kuas muaj tej zaaj tseem ceeb
rua suav dlawg nyeem.  Tab sis yuav tau ua sab luj hab yog tej yaam
kws suav dlawg has ntawd ho paab tej tug.  Tej zaaj mas tsi muaj qaab
hau rua yug tab sis kuj yuav muaj qaab hau rua lwm tug.  Yug tsi nyeem
los ca le xwb yib vim tub tsi muaj leej twg yuam yug.  Tab sis cov sau
los yuav tau sau tej yaam kws zoo rua suav dlawg nyeem.
