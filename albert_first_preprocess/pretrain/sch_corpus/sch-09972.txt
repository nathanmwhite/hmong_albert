Tus resign mas qhia tau hais tias nws yeej tsi hlub nws haiv neeg thiab nws lub tebchaws.
Nws tsi kam muab nws txoj sia los paub nws haiv neeg thiab lub tebchaws.
Thaum muaj kev puam tsuaj tuaj cais nws nrhiav kev ua ntej lawm.

Tus tsi tau resign tiamsis thaum kawg ho tawm tebchaws.
Tus no nws hlub nws lub tebchaws thiab nws haiv neeg dua.
Nws tiv txhua yam txog thaum kawg kiag ua cas los yeej tsi tau lawm nws mas tawm.
Nws tawm tebchaws yam cas pees xwb tsi kam hle lub meej mom thiab lub tebchaws tseg.

Yog yuav muab los luj saib qhov twg tsam teev dua mas tus uas tsi kam resign thiab tiv txog thaum kawg kiag lawm mam tawm thiab yeej tsi kam resign li yog Hmoob tus phaibej.

Tus uas resign ua ntej thiab cia li khiav tawm tseg haiv neeg quaj ntsuag yog neeg huab noj huab haus xwb yeej tsi hlub lub tebchaws thiab pej xeem tiag tiag.
