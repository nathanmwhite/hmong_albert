Xyaum 5 . Teb cov lus nug no .

Neeg yaj sab puas paub fawm qab li cas ?

Vim li cas neeg yaj sab ho noj fawm peb caug feeb twb tsis tas ?

Cov kua fawm qab li cas ?

Koj xav yog vim li cas nws ho nug tias tsam fawm ua nws mob ?

Nws tuaj mus yuav dab tsi hauv nroog ?

Koj xav yog vim li cas nws ho lam yuav tais fawm sim ?

Nyeem rau sau : peb mus tim Hmoob lub peb caug .
Peb pom peb pog pov pob .
Nws pov ib lub pob xim dub dub .
Peb pog kuj ua ib ce zam Hmoob thiab kwv ib lub kaus liab .
Peb mus saib peb pog pov pob .
Nws luag luag rau peb .
Tus yawg Hmoob laus peb pog pov pob rau zoo gaus li twb muaj cuaj caum xyoo lawm .
Vim hais tias nws kam muaj peb ceg diam .
Zoo li nws twb cuam lub pob mus ze ze lawm xwb .
