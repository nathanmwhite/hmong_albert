KEV NT H UAV TAWM Daim ntawv qhia no cov ntsiab lus yog los soj cov kev cai muaj rau cov neeg xiam oob khab txoj kev mus nrhiav kev kho mob .
Nrhiav tau xws li : cov tuam tsev , cov kev pab , thiab cov lus qhia los ntawm cov kws kho mob cov chaw kuaj mob , lwm cov kev kho mob thiab cov ntawv kho mob .
Feem coob ntawm cov neeg nyob hauv lub xeev California , cov kev kho mob mas tej zaum yog tsoom fwv li , xws li lub zej zog , lossis Tswj Kev Kho ( Managed Care ), uas yog ntau qhov chaw kho mob uas nyob rau qab tswj fwm ntawm ib lub tuam txhab loj ( corporation ) lossis cov koos haum tswj ( agency ).
Thaum kho cov neeg siv Medi - Cal , ( Managed Care Organizations ) lawv siv raws li daim ntawv sib cog lus los ntawm cov tsoom fwv ntawm lub xeev thiab / lossis ntawm lub zej zog .
Txhua qhov chaw kho mob txwv tsis pub ua kom saib tsis tau cov neeg xiam oob khab thiab yuav tsum tsim kom muaj ntau txoj kev siv mus los tau zoo .
Tsab cai siv thoob plaws teb chas meskas los tiv thaiv koj cov cai yog cov cai American s with Disabilities Act ( ADA ) thiab Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act ( S ection 504 ).
Cov tsev kho mob uas muaj tswv thiab cov chav kuaj mob uas muaj tswv uas nyob rau Title III ntawm tsab cai ADA vi m lawv yog kev pab rau pej xeem .
Cov tsev kho mob uas yog tsoom fwv li xws li cov zej zog li lossis lwm cov tsev kho mob rau pej xeem , cov chaw kuaj mob thiab cov hoob kas kuaj mob uas nyob rau Title II ntawm tsab cai ADA , vim lawv yog pej xeem ntiag tug .
Section 504 los tswj tau cov tsev kho mob uas tau txais nyiaj txiag los ntawm tsoom fwv tswj teb chaws meskas , xws li Medicare lossis Medi - Cal .
