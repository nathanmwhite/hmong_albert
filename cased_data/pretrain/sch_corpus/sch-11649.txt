HM213 Tejzaum kuj muaj cov tseem yuav mus thiab , sim xyuas mus ntxiv saib .

Nyob lis yus ces cov niag ntawv no yus twb nyem tsi tau lawm es yuav ua cas mus nyob tau lawm mas .
Kuv sij hnov lawv thampem mas tsi muaj ib lub tebchaws yuav zoo nyob tshaj tebchaws nos lawm .
Yog xav nyob kom ntev2 thiab tsis tuag sais mas tebchaws USA nov mas zoo dua .
Yog xav noj zaub mos ces mus kuj zoo thiab tsheej zoo mos2 heev lis .

Cov ua bajsiab yuav mus thov kom ua leb kom zoo2 ua ntej yuav txiav txim siab .

Nyeem tau hab tamsis paub tsi taag tej ntsab lug ntawd lawm .
Has txug qhov sau npe ntawd xwb ces twb ua rua kuv tsi xaav kov nwg le .
Yog moog rua Yob Ntsuagnog lub tebchaws kws tseem yuav tshwm tom plawv havtxwv tuaj , tejzag kuv laam ntxeem kuav tav dlaim nyuas ntawv teev npe ntawd .

Tej nyuas zaub mog ntawd ces laam ua npau - suav xwb .
Nwg tsi laam muaj rua koj noj yooj yim npaum le has ntawd .
Thaus koj moog txug rua tid lawm , ces koj yeej tsi txawv ntawm puab cov ntawd le hab .
Qhov ntawd txhais hastas yuav tau saaj los yeej yog qhov nyuab lawm .
Nwg yeej kawg rovqaab ntsuas kaaj nyuas zaub qaub laug ntawd xwb .
