Txhua yam tsis mus raws li qhov sawvdaws kwvyees tseg . Xyoo no , luag
tsis sau nyiaj nram chaw nres tsheb ( fair ground ), tabsis sau nyiaj
($4 per person , age 4 - 64 ) pem rooj vag yuav nkag mus rau hauv lub
tshav nquam paj nquam nruas xwb . Hos tej kev ntawm ib ncig ze los yeej
pub nres tsheb , tabsis tsuas pub nres rau ib sab kev xwb . Qhov luag ua
li no yog ua kom cov tsheb mus los sib zam tau yoojyim xwb . Qhov kuv
pom hais tias tsis tshua zoo mas yog cov bus uas thauj neeg rov los
nram fair ground thaum yav lig (~ 8 : 00 PM ) ntawd tsawg dhau heev lawm .
Qhov uas yuav muab ob lub events los sb piv mas yus kuj tsis pom qab
piv lawm . Raws li kuv soj ntsuam mas Moob cov events no yeej yog
pheejyig rau cov pejxeem heev li . Qhov txawv tshaj luag lwm lub event
mas cov rooj muag khoom no Moob muab sau kim dhau heev lawm ($500 -
$1000 / booth ). Zoo qhov uas peb cov tub lag tub luam tsis xam lawv
lub nqi dag nqi zog hlo li , tsis li ntawd mas ntshe cov event no yuav
mus tsis taus kiag . Hos nyob li luag lwm lub event mas laug sau nqi
rooj pheejyig , tabsis sau cov neeg tuaj koom kim ces kuj cover cov
expense lawm thiab . Pivtxwv li lub EAA event nyob Oshkosh ( airplane
show ), luag sau nqi nres tsheb yog $10/car , sau nqi booth $50/booth ,
sau nqi neeg nkag $35/person .
