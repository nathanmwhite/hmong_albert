Peb moob tub ua kag le ntawm lawm neb , muaj newhom , newhom has tas nwg
txhaj heev hab txhaj coj tau moob xwb , muaj caub fab , caub fab tas
puab txhaj heev hab txhaj ua tau .  Muaj hmong world congress , puab tas
puab txhaj yog paab tseem nkaag tau rua UN .  Muaj DYD paab hab tas cov
cov txawj ntse mas txhaj has tau nkaag kij rua nplajteb .  Haub yab zoo
le nuav es ua dlaab tsi los txhaj tsi tav le ntaag lauj .  Txhaj maag
nplog muaj tua ua nyuv tawm ruag tsi xwb tag lauj .

tub naj nub ua le lawm nej .  Hamas tas puab txhaj yog paab haib mas
presaident abba yuav tsum tawm pab tha , puab txhaj muab nwg paab laij
tawm huv gaza moog lawm .  VP has tas nwg txhaj yog thawj coj mas leej
twg tsi yuav le nwg has cais nwg yuav muab foom kuam tuag moog .  Ab
