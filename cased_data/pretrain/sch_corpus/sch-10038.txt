Peb txiv huabtais vaajpov has tas nwg muaj peb yaam xwb kws yuav ua
rua Hmoob sib koom tau ua ke .
1 .  Hmoob Vaaj yuav tsu ua thawj coj xwb tsi pub lwm xeem ua hlo le
2 .  Yuav tsum siv tuab tug ntawv xwb kws yog tug peb txiv huabtais
vaaj pov pum zoo lossis cov vaaj pum zoo
3 .  Yuav tsum koom tuab tug kaasmoos xwb kws yog tug peb txiv huabtais
vaaj pov tsaa .
Yog txhua yaam kws tsi nkaag rua peb nqe nuav cais yeej coj tsi tau
hmoob lug sib koom ib zag .  Txawm lwm xeem hmoob koom taag lawm los
peb cov vaaj luj , vaaj miv nuav yeej tsi koom hlo le .
Peb cov vaaj yuav tau paub qhov nuav zoo zoo nawb .  Tug vaaj twg tseem
moog koom lwm qhov cais tug ntawm tsi yog vaaj tabsis yog vaaj tug
yeeb ncuab .
