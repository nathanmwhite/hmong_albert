Yuav tsum hais tias merry Satan ( yawm ntxwg nyoog ) no mas thiaj yog Hmoob li poj ua tseg yawg ua cia .

Nco ntsoov tias dab ntxwg nyoog thiaj yog Hmoob tug tiamsis tus Hmoob twg ua muab yawg ntxwg nyoog tso tseg lawm mas yuav tau koob hmoov heev .

Nco ntsoo ( Merry Satanism and Happy New Year ) thiaj yog Hmoob li 3 hmoob ruam , yeej ruam tiag .
