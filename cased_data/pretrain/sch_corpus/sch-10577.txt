Hmoob txoj kev sib hlub yog sib hlub ntawm lub ncauj xwb tsis yog sib hlub hauv lub siab puas yog ?

Npawg , Hmoob kev sib hlub muaj tabsis tsuas yog xav xwb tsis tau muab los coj .
Ib yam li Hmoob ntsaw koj muaj unity , tabsis tsuas yog ntshaw xwb never pib unite .
Thaum tham tau tawm lawm ces pib ntawm koj thiab ntawm kuv ntag .
