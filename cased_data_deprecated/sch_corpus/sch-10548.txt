1 .  Yog koj yuav coj ib tug txiv neej uas tsis muaj los tsis coj poj

tsum tau nrhiav ib tug " date " rau nws tso es sawvdaws mam mus ua ke .

just a " companion " for that particular function .
2 . Yog koj yuav cav las voos rau tus txiv neej uas tsis coj/muaj
pojniam tuaj nrog , koj tsis txhob nrhiav pojniam rau nws .  Yog nws
tsis muaj ces nws mam thov lwm tug mus nws tug tsis li ntawd los nws
mam nrhiav nws xwb .  Yog koj nrhiav ib tug pojniam los nrog nws
lasvoos mas raws li kuv xav qhov no yog saib tsis taus tus pojniam/
hluas nkauj ntawd ntag ( 80%) hos 20% mas yog saib tsis taus yawm thaub
ntawd vim nws tsis " muaj khua xam maj " nrhiav tus dancing partner .
There is no " honor " or " muaj kiaj " in getting a woman or young girl to
dance with a total stranger .
3 . Yog koj ob niam txiv coj ib tug yawm tsis coj pojniam tuaj mus tom
party , you - the husband must be prepared to do 2 things : a ) find him
a partner/date OR 2 ) accept the fact that your wife would be his
dancing partner too .  Yog li ntawd mas neb 2 niam txiv yuav tsum sib
tham tej no kom nkag siab ua ntej nej yuav coj tus neeg ntawd mus
party nrog neb .
