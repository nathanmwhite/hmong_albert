Yog lawm .  Peb moob tsuas has nrhav kev txawjntse lug caijtsuj hab
kwhv noj cov ruam .  Hab peb nrhav kev phem lug tawmtsaam kuas yeej lwm
tug .  Qhov ntawd yog peb moob tug cwjpwm ( paradigm ) kws muaj ntev heev
lawm .
Has txug tev zoo mas peb yeej los tsi muaj tuabneeg tuaj thaam .  Has
txug kev phem mas coov heev .  Qhov nuav yog peb qhov txuj lw
( paradigm ) kws peb kwv ntawm teg hab noj ntawm ncauj xwb ( short
