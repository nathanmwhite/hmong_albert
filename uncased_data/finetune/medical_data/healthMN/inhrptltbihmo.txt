yog tias kuv tsis noj tshuaj ne?
yog koj tsis noj inh-rpt, ncua ntau hnub, los sis tsum tsis noj tshuaj ua ntej koj tus kws kho mob los sis nurse hais kom tsum, koj kuj yuav mob tau tus kab mob act ive tb.
nws tseem ceeb yuav tsum tua cov me nyuam kab mob tb me me kom koj thiab koj tsev neeg thiaj tsuas muaj noj qab nyob zoo.
yog tias kuv twb tau txhaj koob bcg lawm ne?
bcg tiv thaiv me nyuam yaus kom txhob raug tej hom tb uas mob hnyav tiamsis tsuas kav ob peb xyoo xwb.
nws tiv thaiv tsis tau ltbi los sis kab mob active tb.
yog koj twb tau txhaj koob bcg, los koj yuav tsum noj tshuaj rau ltbi kom tiv thaiv koj tus kheej thiab lwm tus.
yog tias kuv muas tsis tau inh-rpt ne?
