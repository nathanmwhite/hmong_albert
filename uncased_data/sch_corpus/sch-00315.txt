tsi yog niam tais ntsuab...this word is used because mauj ib co tsi paub xwb ces lawd cia li lam hais, and then the ones that really dont know...start using it....lo lus tseem/tseeb mas yog "niam txais ntsuab"--in esscence  yog ua tshoob loj loj, ces muaj niam txais ntsuab txiv txais ntsuab...the receiving couple..and muaj niam xa txiv xa..the "giving" couple.
as for "bridesmaid"...the term is "niam ua luag"...niam ua luag is and can be used for bridesmaid.  however, niam ua luag is not the same as niam txais ntsuab (two different entities).
