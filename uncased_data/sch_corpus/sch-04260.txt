muaj ib yawg saub hmoob hais rau kuv tias thaum lawv ua rog caubfab
nyob nplogteb mas yeej muaj tus cawm seej los pab lawv tiag tiag li,
rau qhov thaum ntawm hmoob raug kev txom nyem.  tiamsis txij li thaum
hmoob tsa muaj nom muaj tswv, muaj fuabtais vaj yim leej, muaj coos
phoos, coos luaj ntau ntau tuaj cais tus pab ntawm cia li tsi los pab
lawm.  muaj ib hmov tus pab ntawm tau los ua yog toog rau tus saub
ntawm tias nws yuav tsi muaj peev xwm los pab lawv ntxiv lawm, rau
qhov lawv twb yog vaj yim leej, yog coos phoos, coos luaj lawm.  yog
li tus pab ntawm lub neej mom twb tsi yog vaj yim leej lossis coos
phoos coos luaj li lawv lawm.  lawv yeej muaj lub meej mom thiab
hwjchim los txaus yuav cawm hmoob lawv.  cais txij lis ntawm los hmoob
caubfab cia li tsi khoo thiab raug tua tuag ua lwj ua liam los txog
sawvdaws tawg tas.  thaum twg hmoob muab hmoob txhawb rau qhov chaw
siab cais hmoob yuav puam tsuaj.  txij lis peb muaj huabtais hmoob
nyob tebchaws america cais peb txawm cia li pib rov qab tuaj tom tw
lawm tiag.  cia luag tsa yus mas thiaj kav ntev hos yug tsa yus mas
kawg tuaj tom tw swb tiag cov yawg.

kuv xaav tas yog le cais thaum cov moob dlawb puab khaav tas puab zoo
dlua moob leeg cais puab txhaj maag ua ntxhuv nyo puas yog.
