yog taagnrho le tej laug has:
1. npam - muaj nyob rua huv rojntshaav kws tej laug pum tej yaam phem ntawd tshwm rua tam 3-4
2. foom - kev xaav lug ntawm kev chim sab kws tshev kuas mob-mob lawv le tej kws moob twb pum dlua lug lawm.
3. dlaag - kuj yog dlaag hab vim tsi yog txhua tam losyog txhua tug tuabneeg kws raug foom phem yuav npam taag txhua tug
4. tag - yeej muaj dlua lug lawm es tej laug txhajle has tawm (nqe 1 sautoj ntawd)
yog cov ntxhais phem / zoo lug ntawm tej yawm koob lug.
yog cov tub phem / aoo lug ntawm tej puj koob lug.
