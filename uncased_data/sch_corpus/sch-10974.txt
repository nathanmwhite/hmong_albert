khib puas zoo thiab?  khib puas yog hlub?  khib puas tau?  yog tau,
yuav khib txog qib twg?  yog tsis tau, vim li cas?  yog tsis sib khib
kiag yog txhais li cas?

khib puas zoo thiab?  
tsis zoo.  vim tias "khib" ua rau yus mob siab heev.  kev mob siab
tsis yog ib qho zoo.  yog cheem tau mas tsis txhob khib zoo dua.
khib puas yog hlub?  
tsis yog.  khib yog vim ntshai tsam plam kev hlub.  khib tsis yog
hlub.
khib puas tau?  
xyov koj nug li cas?
1.  puas yog koj nug tias koj puas muaj cai khib?
koj muaj cai khib los mas.  yeej tsis muaj tibneeg cheem koj.
2.  los yog koj nug tias yog koj khib es puas yuav ua tau rau koj tau
nws?
yog tau, yuav khib txog qib twg?  yog tsis tau, vim li cas?  
2.  koj txawm khib los qhov txiav txim tias koj tau thiab tsis tau nws
no nyob ntawm nws, tsis yog nyob ntawm koj.
yog tsis sib khib kiag yog txhais li cas?
yog tsis sib khib kiag ces txhais tau tias:
1.  neb yeej sib hlub sib ntseeg txaus lawm.  your love for one each
other is unquestionable.
2.  neb yeej tsis sib hlub kiag li.  you don't give a damn what
happens to the other person because your heart is elsewhere.
