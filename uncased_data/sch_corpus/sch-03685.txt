tseemyeej thiab tshua,
neb mas sib phim kawg kiag li nawb. hais rau neb paub tias peb qhov
muag yeej tsis ntsais kiag li no ov. saib saib zaum no leej twg yuav
mag chob kub kwv hlo no, tsis txhob lam dhia tawm cus cus nawb. cuab
tau taw lawm yeej meem laub kom kawg ov mog.

tshua,
koj mas txawj zes taws kawg rau koj tus yawg laus tseemyeej yom. txog
sia koj nawb, kav tsij yim meem siv zog tiag ov. kuv tuaj hauv no los
yog vim xav saib neb ua pa av ncho lug li no xwb tiag.
tseemyeej, koj tus me tub tshua no mas zoo li luag hlob tiav txiv lawm
ces thaum ntej mas luag lam yoj yoj kub sim ua si xwb tiam sis thaum
luag sim mus sim tuaj nas has luag paub tias yog luag siv zog laub,
luag yuav yeej no ces luag thiaj pheej tso pam thawj tshab kub xwb. nco
ntsoov tias koj yeej ib txwm yog tus champ kav hauv no, yuav tau saib
kom meej pem nawb tsam tej yau ho lam ntxeev siab laiv.
