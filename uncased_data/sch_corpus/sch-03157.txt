destructive. peb txawm yuav yog moob leeg, hmoob dawb, hmoob dab tsi

do we feel when we are discriminated as being "chinese? nigger? etc.."

why don't we do our homework by going back, say, 5000 or more years
and ask the question "why so many names have arisen for a so small
group of people like us? what were the forces that made these changes
possible? was it possible to be war that made this possible? was it
because the hmong/moob were equally ignorant and equally
discriminatory as we are today? do we or should we feel ashamed to
have been happily and proudly denounced our very own brothers and
sisters to the point of "none-senseness"? do we or will we ever have
time or make time to build love and lasting relationship between all
the names i mentioned at the beginning ( and still more that we don't
yet know)?
it will be wiser for us all to discover our true past before we will
be happy to waste our precious time to discriminate against one
another. like the movie of the lion king. no matter what, "we are
one!"
