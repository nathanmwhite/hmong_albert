hmoob tso tsis tau huabtais
1976 thaum kuv mus nrog tojxeem xaiv suav yaj lawv nyob naj mub (pha
khao) mas cov hmoob nyob rau sab phwv ciab, phwv los pheej, naj kaib,
thaj kub lub, pha ngang, phuaj thoos phuaj vis, thiab tshav twm xeev
luang prabang sawv ua chao fa coob coob tawm tsam tseemfwv nplogliab
(lprd).  xov cav nroog nreb hais tias hmoob nyob rau sab luang prabang
tau mus tso huabtais nplog tiao sisavang vatthana.
1986 -87 kuv thiaj ua research thiab nug cov hmoob chao fa phou luang
prabang uas khiav tuaj rau chiang kham & vinai, thaibteb thiab tuaj
amelicas teb no txog ntawm qhov lawv (hmoob) mus tso huabtais nplog
tiao sisavang vatthana.  yawg ntxoov qiam yaj tus haus zos (nai ban)
tsav twm, xeev luang prabang uas tawgrog tuaj nyob california thiab
vam yig xyooj phou chia uas tawgrog tuaj nyob minnesota. kuv pom hais
tias tsim nyog cov phoojywg pojntxoog hauv sch no yuav tsum tau twm.
yawg ntxov qiam yaj hais tias,  december, 1976 former royal lao air
force col. somphone thiab tiao fasay vongsavang tau tuaj ntsib cov
chao fa yawg ntxoov qiam yaj tus hauv zos zos tsav twm. tiao fasay
vongsavang hais tias, thov nej cov hmoob tuaj pab tso saib puab tau
txiv huabtais tawm.
tom qab sib ntsib tag col. somphone thiab tiao fasay vongsavang coj
cov lus uas tau nrog peb sib tham mus hais (hawm) vajntxwv tiao
sisavang vathana.  huabtais thiaj sau ib tsab ntawv tuaj rau peb hais
tias “nej cov menyuam hmoob, kuv txiv huabtais pom zoo li nej npaj
thiab thov nej tuaj tso kuv tawm.  nej tej txiaj ntsig yuav tsis lwj,
kuv yuav pauj kom tau thaum tseem muaj sia nyob, thiab kuv tej
xeebleej xeebntxwv yuav nco ntxoov tsis kawg.  thov kom ua tau li nej
npaj”.
