yeeb ceeb cais yog cov uas los tso dej thaum tibneeg pw raug zog lawm thiab los kov tais kov ntiav thaum tib neeg tsi nyob lossis pw lawm.

yaj ceeb yog cov uas pheej coj yeeb ceeb tus plig mus muag rau dab qus thiab pheej coj mus tuaj no.
yeeb ceeb mas saib tsi pom thiab cov yuav pom tau cais yog us ntsujplig lawm mam li pom xwb 1/22/09 yaj ceeb ces yog sawvdaws lub neej niaj hnub noj nyob no, ho yeej ceeb ces yog thaum yus pw tsaug zog es ua npau suav.

tawg paj saum yaj txiv txiv nruab yeeb.
yaj ceeb yeeb ceeb los sib koom ua ke thiaj txawm muaj noob neej, noob tsia, noob xyoob, noob ntoo, ua rau lub universe no muaj txhua yam.

kaj ntug pom kev yog yaj.
tsaus ntuj txias to yog yeeb.
