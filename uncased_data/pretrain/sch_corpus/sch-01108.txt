cov yawm,  thaus moog "yuav mus baum nyub plaub, rov los baum nyuj
kub", le has ntawd, rua sau neej ces moob tawg sau roob coob heev tuaj
moog rua thaib teb lawm.  thaus kawg, "vwj mai" hab tug puav moob ho
rovqaab moog rua nplog teb lawm xwb.  (remember, it was spoken in the
stance of laos).
dlhau le ntawd kuj paub tsi txug tej kws tseem yuav tshwm losyog "npau-
suav" ntawd xwb vim hastas, nwg tsi yog has ib tug tuabneeg twg kws
yog "tony, cwjmem, xyeejtsawg, losyog idonotknow" tamsis nwg has txug
"moob" as a people.  ntxiv moog ru qhov ntawd, nwg hastxug the realm
of the spirit kws khaubzig tuaj rua sau neej.  nyob rua saab ntuj nrau
u (saab ntsujplig) nyaj yuav muaj le ntawd, tamsis saab ntuj nrau nuav
(sau neej) yuav tsi muaj le ntawd.  txawm muaj los yuav tsi yog le
koj, kuv, hab peb pum ntawm lub qhovmuag.  nwg yog txuj lw (over time)
kws qha txug yug haiv moob lub neej kws hlaa dlha caij dlhau nyoog
(space and time) ntawd xwb.
yog mej leej twg tsi tau moog saib meskas dlaim yeebyaajkab, "the
immortal" kws tseem taabtom ua ntawd, nyog zoo thooj le saub ntsuabxwm
cov lug ntawd.
dlhau ntawd cov tej tug psychic kuj hastxug hastas saab ntsujplig (the
other side = the realm of the spirit) kuj muaj toj roob hauvpeg,
xyoobntooj, dlejnaam, tuabneeg (spirit) le sau neej nuav hab).  rov
qaab moog noog txug leej txiv kws moog nrhav nwg tug tub es nwg ho
tuag moog txug rua "sau ntuj" ntawd lawm.  (notice that, he was no
long in the realm of the physical being but in the spirit)  it is
deceiving of not knowing how to separate the realm of the spirit from
the physical world or vise verso which drives people nuts (crazy or
insane).
