kuv yeej pum tas peb hmoob muab hlub menyuam dlhau hab xaav kuam puab tsim txaj ua neeg zoo muaj koob muaj npe.
peb qhov kev nrhaw nuav ua rua peb muaj espectation luj heev rua peb cov menyuam.
tabsis cov nam menyuam tebchaws nuav mas tawv le tawv hab phem le phem yeej qha tsi tau has tsi yuav le.
thaum zoo le nuav cais peb paub tas tsi qhuab tag yeej yuav tsi tsim txaj tag tag le cais muaj ib cov txhaj le siv txhua yaam lug tswj saib puas hloov tau.
thaum kawg txhaj zoo le nuav.

peb yuav tau txhob muaj espectation sab sab rua cov nom menyuam ntawm hab.
qha le yug qha tau tau xwb yog nwg yuav los yuav tsi yuav los kaav lam.
thaum nwg txom nyem los yog nwg teeb meem, yog ho moog phov phov es tuag lawm los kawg faus.
yog tsheb nrau tuag los lwm tug tua tuag kuj ho tau mentsis insurance zoo dlua xaav xaav kuam puab zoo es yug maag txim.
yog yug yuav raug txim kuj ca nwg raug es yug dlim.
cov menyuam tebchaws nuav mas tsawg tug txhaj ncu yug ua nam ua txiv tej txaj ntsig os.
