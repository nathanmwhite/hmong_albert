even in the early 17th and 18th century, hmong already graduated from
chinese school.  hmong yeej ib txwm tsi ruam li txhua tus hais tag los
lawm.  hmoob yeej ib txwm ntse, mus saib es zoosiab rau koj tus kheej
es sivzog tiag tiag hmoob yuav muaj hnub zoo ntxiv xwb.

nws lub npe nom yog vwj paj cai
cov hmoob xeev uas nrog fabkis tawm tsam lawv haiv neeg hmoob lam hais
tias nws vwm xwb, qhov tseeb tiag nws tsis npab.  nws pom tau lawm tias
fabkis tuaj caij tsuj peb hmoob.  yim twg tsis muaj nyiaj them se,
fabkis tuaj muab cov menyuam mus muag los them.  vim phem npaum no,
hmoob thiaj li sawv tua fabkis.  thov kom peb sawvdaws nco qab tias peb
hmoob muaj peev xwm npaum li cas.  riam phom los yog hmoob ua hmoob,
tsis tos lwm haiv neeg tuaj pab.  ua rog ua rau fabkis ntshai tag lawm.
tiamsis, li nej yeej hnov lawm, hmoob tsis txawj hlub hmoob, hmoob rov
xuas taw tuam ntuj, rov qab muab tus coj tua.  tom qab no, fabkis thiaj
li tua yeej, thiaj li tuaj txhom cov tub rog mus tua rau xeev.  nug nej
niam nej txiv lawv, lawv yeej paub txog tej no.

qhov tseeb ces peb ib kuag yeej paub tias vwj paj cai yog peb hmoob ib
