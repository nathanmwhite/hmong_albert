lu lug "plaub" yog 4 txuj cai luj rau txim.
thaum u, vaaj hmoob tau tsaa 4 txuj cai luj lug kaav hmoob xws: 1) qha--txhum zag ib raug qha; 2) qhuab--txhum zag ob raug qhuab; 3) cuab--txhum zag peb raug cuab; 4) tua--txhum zag plaub raug tua.
vim le ntawd thaum ib tug twg ua txhum ib qho lawm txha has tas txhum plaub, los yog txhum 4 txuj cai luj kws muaj tseg lawm.

lu lug "ntug" lug ntawm lu "ntuj" txhais has tas "luj".

"plaub ntug" txhais has tas 4 txuj cai luj.
tej zag yuav tsi yog le cov lug kws koj nov ntawd.
