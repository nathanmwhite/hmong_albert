tug laug has tas ua le ntawd mas tsi khov kag le.

ob tug hluas, ib tug laug tuaj sis twg ua tuam thawj tswj koom hum, puab peb leeg has peb zaaj lug saib puab yuav tuav lub koom hum le caag.

ob tug cov ntsab lug muaj kuab zoo.
tug laug has lug nldaug dlua.
nwg cov ntsab lug muaj kuab tsi npau ob tug hluas le.
thaus xaiv tug laug tau lawm.

2. puab twb yeej xaiv tom tsev tuaj lawm los yog puab xaiv tom qaab puab noog peb zaaj lug kws ob tug hluas hab ib tug laug tuaj moog sis tw ntawd?
