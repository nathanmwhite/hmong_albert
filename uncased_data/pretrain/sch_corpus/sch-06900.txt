there is no restrictions on age.
my youngest student is 9. when he started, he was 7...
right now, he's playing at funerals (nws tsi tau ua tau txiv qeej).
i'm expecting him to be ready for txiv qeej role at about 11 or by next year.
my youngest student to be a txiv qeej is 15.

he started when he was 13. my oldest student to be txiv qeej is older than i am (he's 40).
as for myself, i didnt' start until i was in high school.
pib ua txiv qeej around 18...
but of course, i have been helping my father for years before txiv qeej role.

hais txog ua txiv qeej ces, age is not an issue.
however, muaj ib cov qeej hu ua "qeej nruam/tw luav" and "niam xub/pluag" for qeej pam tuag.

cov qeej no yog qeej foom, tej laus yeej hais tias "yog tsi tau muaj nyab or tsi tau qua ntxhais," ces tsi zoo tshuab cov qeej no rau xyom cuab.
yus txawm txawj npaum cas los yog txog hnub ua qhua lawm, yog tias yus tseem hluas heev ces yus xob tshuab cov qeej no.
similar to txiv xaiv, yog tias yus lub neej tsi tau tiav, yus tsi zoo ua tus thawj txiv xaiv.
yog yus pab xwb los tau, temsis ua tsi tau tus thawj.
hais txog qeej los yus tshuab tau qeej dog dig xwb, xob tshuab qeej foom.
