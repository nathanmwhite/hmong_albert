tos peb hmoob pheej nyiam-m mus tua nas hauv private av.....
nas paub tias khiav rau hauv private av ces tsis muaj neeg caum tua lawm....
tab sis hmoob txhob pom xwb....
hmoob yeej tsis paub tias yog private av li koj hais li nawb.

xaj maim 1983-1995 ntawd nyob rau wisconsin mas nas coob heev, thaum sawv ntxov tsees mas ceg ntoo co qig txag xwb.
yus mus tua nas tsis tag yuav mus tag hnub yeej tau txaus lawm tiam sis ua li nej hais, hmoob tuaj coob zuj zus ces hmoob cia li muab nas tua tuag tag.
yuav ua li cas nas tsis tu noob mas, tua ib zaug tsis yog tua kom taus li luag limit xwb tiam sis ua kom puv hnab khib nyiab xwb ces me niam nas txiv nas yeej kawg tu noob tiag laus.
ziag no mas hav zoov nqhuab npo tshua tus puav chipmonk lawm xwb.
muaj ib yawg phub thaub tham tham rau kuv tias muaj ib xyoo lawv mus tua nas, lawv tua tau ntau heev thiab lawv tseem tua tau ib tug turkey nrog thiab.
lawv muab faus nplooj lwg rau hauv ib qho hav zawj ze ntawm lawv cov tsheb ces ziag no ranger txawm tuaj kuaj lawv.
ranger coj ib tug dev thiab thaum tuaj txog ranger nrog lawv tham tham tias lawv tua puas tau, lawv tias lawv tua tau ob peb tug xwb--ranger tus dev mas dhia cus loj cus les yuav pus ranger mus rau tom pawg nas lawv muab faus tab sis hmoov kuj muaj ranger muab nws cab rov los rau tom luv lawm xwb, lawv mas ib ce tuag tag.
yog ranger cia nws tus dev mus hlib mas ntshai lawv yuav mag txim loj heev.
