luas lwm tug tuabneeg tej ncaulug.
qheb sab yog thws (txu) qhov sab losyog nthuab lub sab lug rua
suavdlawg pum.
1.  thawj zaaj lug (first dragon word) has yog lawm.
2.  zaaj lug ob (dragon word two) has yog lawm.
3.  zaaj ib hab zaaj ob (dragon one and dragon two) puavleej yog.
4.  zaaj ib (dragon one) has yog dlua.

hastas, "ua le caag moob yuav muaj lulug "sab" ntawd ntau ualuaj le?"
