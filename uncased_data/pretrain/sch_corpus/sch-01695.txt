cov phoojywg tub yos tsiaj, twb yuav rov txog lub caij peb mus plob havzoov tua muaslwj dua lawm.

nyob rau wi yog lub xeev uas peb tau muaj tej xwm txheej tsis zoo los raug ntau zaus rau lub caij yos.
zaum xwmtxheej uas loj tshaj uas novqab tsis tau kiag yom zaum nchiav suav tau tua cov tub yos tsiaj tuag lawm 6 leej.
yog nchaiv suav lam kawm kom paub thajtsam yos kom zoo, yog nchaiv suav lam ua siab ntev zog, es nyaj tej xwm txheej no yuav tsis tshwmsim loj npaum ntawd.

yog li kuv thov nqua hu peb ib tseem tubyos kom sawvdaws yuav tau xyuamxim, yuav tau rub lub siab kom ntev, yuav tau kawm kom paub thaj chaw yus yos kom zoo zoo.
dhau li ntawd, yuav tau kawm kom paub tus cai yos havzoov lossis yos muaslwj kom zoo zoo, thiaj tsis ua txhaum luag txoj cai.

kawm kom paub koj thaj chaw yos kom zoo (visit this dnr map site): http://www.website.net/org/land/facilities/dnr_lands_mapping_search.asp?

kawm kom paub txojcai yos kom zoo (see hmong version hunting regulation).

http://www.website.net/org/land/wildlife/regs/deer10_hmong.pdf good luck and hunt safely, hl yog lawm nawb ib tsoom kwvtij hmoob, peb koom nrog luag yo hav zoov ua ke yuav tau rub lub siab kom ntev luag txawm cem yus khib me ntsis los yog yus zam ces yeej dhau txhob pub tej xwm txheej no tshwm sim ntxiv lawm luag thiaj tsis saib peb hmoob phem phem.
txoj kev yos hav zoov luag tsoomfwv yeej pub txhua tus muaj cai yos ib yam, peb yeej yuav tau ntsib tus neeg phem thiab zoo ua ke yeej meem ua siab ntev ntshe ho yog accident lawm xwb.
