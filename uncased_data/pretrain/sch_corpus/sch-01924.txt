caijnyoog – tense-time (npe) qhatxug hastas nwg yog yaav taaglug, taamsim nuav, losyog yaav pegsuab.

faabtawgntso – going on (npe) qhatxug hastas ib tug tuabneeg tseem taabtom leg ib teg dlejnum rua lub caijnyoog taamsim ntawd.

hloovnpe – pronoun (npe) qhatxug ib lulug kws siv lug hloov lub npe tuabneeg, npe nroog, losyog tej npe kws nchaa moo ntawd chaw.

ibtxwm coj – tradition (npe) qhatxug hastas ib paab tuabneeg yeej nyob lubneej zoo lentawd ib tam dlhau moog rua ib tam.

keebkwm – history (npe) qhatxug lub hauvpaug ntawm ib tug, ib paab, losyog ib haivtuabneeg lug ntawm nwg lub chaw yug, thaaj chaw nrhavnoj nrhavhaus, hab lub chaws xaus ntawm lubneej.

khoomsiv – object (npe) qhatxug hastas nwg yog tej tswjsiv xws le taig, dlav, rooj, tog, vaajtsev, hab tsheb.

ntu (npe) qhatxug hastas nwg yog ibkem ntawm ib tug dlej, ib paab nuabneeg, losyog ib lub neej.

tsaamthawj (npe) qhatxug hastas lub caij ntawm muaj ntau yaam tshwm tuaj rua huv losyog muaj kev txawvtxaav ntau yaam rua lub caijnyoog ntawd.

ncua (npe) qhatxug hastas nwg yog ib kem, ib ntu, losyog ib theem ntawm ib ntog kev, ib thaaj chaw, ib txuj kaab, lub neej, losyog keebkwm.
