cov phoojywg, tsis yog nej xwb, kuv los yeej ntaus yuam kev tib yam.
yog yus tsis ua twb zoo muab los nyeem thiab kho ua ntej mam xa tawm mas haj yam yuam kev ntau.
hos tab yus twb muab nyeem thiab kho ob peb lwm lawm los lwm zaus yus tseem pom qhov yuam kev dua.
tsis hais ntaus ntawv hmoob los ntawv english, yog yus ntau ntaus kam lawm ces cia li ntau tsuag lawm xwb.
yus lub siab xav tau lo lus xwb ces yus cov ntiv tes twb muab ntaus tag lawm.
muaj tej thaum yus cov ntiv tes nrawm zog lawm ces thiaj ntaus tau yuav kev rhuav li no ntag.
tej thaum mas yog maj zog ces yus ntaus ceev zog ces thiaj cia li ntaus tau yuam kev lawm xwb.

nej sawvdaws hais puav leej yog lus.
yeej muaj feem hais tias vim yus tsis niaj hnub siv, thiab yeej muaj feem hais tias vim yog tus ntawd rpa no siv tib co alphabet li tus ntawd english es peb thiaj pheej ntaus yuam kev.
thaum peb ntaus ua lus english los yeej yuam kev kawg thiab, tabsis yog nej qhov software muaj autmatic spelling correction, ces nej ntaus tau yuam kev me me los nws muab kho kiag rau lawm ces yam li yus txawm ntaus tau yog yog xwb.
yav tom ntej no yuav muaj cov los txhim kho kom muaj qhov software los self check thiag automatic corrections peb cov ntawv hmoob no thiab.

yog li koj hais lawm, thiab cov ntawv hmoob no tsis yog kawm hauv tsev kawm ntawv los es niaj hnub kawb, hos kawm tom havzoo los xwb ces nco tsis zoo.

mej puavleej has yog taagnrho huvtuabsi: 1. peb sau ntawv le peb has lug, ces peb tseem pheej xaav pheej sau.

2. thaus peb sau le peb has lug, ces peb feeb tsi tshuam meej lawm vim yog hastas peb tseem xaav hastas yuav sau lecaag.

3. thaus peb tseem xaav, peb lub sab tseem nrhav lug, peb tug ntivteg twb ntaus ntawv uantej lawm.

yog le ntawd, thaus peb muaj lub sab ntshai nyob tsaam peb ntaus yuamkev, thaus ntawd ces peb nyeem los yeej tsi tshuas pum lulug yuamkev ntawd lawm.
thaus peb lub sab tug hab tsi txhawj lawm peb nyeem ces peb txhajle pum lulug yuamkev ntawd meej.

sau ntawv le peb has lug, has lug le peb sau ntawv yog ib txujkev kws peb yuav tshuaj tau peb tej kev sauntawv kuas meej.
yog peb muaj ib tug qauv losyog standard lawm ces peb txawm yuav xaav, xaam, nrhav, ntaus, hab maaj npaum lecaag los tug qauv ntawd yuav ncua yug lub hlwb, tug ntivteg, txheejtxheem sau.
thaus yug paub meej lawm, yug tsi muaj kev maaj hab kev ntshai ces yug yuavtsum sau yog xwb.
