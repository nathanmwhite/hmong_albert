li nram qab no:
1.   cov nplog uas yog pro-chinese, pro-vietnamese, pro-thaib & pro-
western yog leej twg?
-cov nplog qabteb thiab nplog hnub tuaj (southern & eastern) yog pro-
vietnamese.  cov nplog qaumteb (northern laos) yog pro-chinese.  cov
pro-thaib thiab pro-western yog cov pejxeem, cov tub lag tub luam, yop
cov nplog dawb (nplog tog vientiane), yog cov nplog uas nyob raws tus
ntuj dej naj khoom (mekong river) thiab yog cov nplog uas muaj kwvtij
neejtsa khiav tawm rau lwm tebchaws lawm.
-pro-vietnamese: kaysone phomvihane yog neeg savannakhet, khamtay
siphandone yog neeg pakse, choummaly sayasone yog neeg attapu,
bouasone boudphavanh yog neeg saravane,  nouhak phomsavanh yog neeg
kham muam (nouhak phoumsavane yug nyob ban phalouka, chanvad mukdahan,
thailand}.  nouhak phomsavane yog neeg nyablaj (lao vietnamese).
-pro-chinese:  somsavad lengsavad neeg luang prabang, asang laoly neeg
phomsaly, dr. bounpoune bouttannavong neeg oudonesay, nrog rau cov qub
nom neo lao hat sak uas tuag tag lawm cov neeg.
2.   leej twg (who) yuav los ua prime minister of lprd tom qab
thongsing thammavong?
-raws li xav mas tsis yog lt. gen. douangchay phichit mas yuav tsum
yog dr. thongloun sisoulith.  dr. thounloun sisoulith yog member  hauv
koum kanmuang sounkan phak thib 4, lt. gen. douangchay phichit yog
thib 6.  tabsi vim dr. thongloun yog neeg sam neua thiab paub systems
coj noj coj ua cov twbchaws western zoo mas phak pasason pathivat lao
yuav tsis lam zam kev rau dr. thongloun sisoulith ua.  payna yathotou
yog ntxhais hmoob yuav txiv nplog luag (nplog) yuav tsis lam cia pany
ua ib zaug.
3.   kaysone phonevihane puas muaj tub?
-kasone muaj tub coob tug tabsi tus uas nto koob tshaj yog dr.
xaysomphone phomvihane.  tam sim no dr. xaysomphone phomvihane yog tus
vice president of lao national assembly thiab yog member of central
committee # 17.
4.   tiao souphanouvong tsevneeg puas tseem muaj cov nyob nplogteb?
-muaj, souphanouvong tsevneeg tseem nyob nplogteb.  khamsay
souphanouvong tibleeg thiaj khiav tawm tuaj mus rau new zealand thaum
xyoo 2000 xwb, tag cov tseem nyob nplogteb.  tamsi no (2012) tiao
souphanouvong ib tug tub dr. douangsavad souphanouvong tau los ua
minister for the prime minister office (minister for the government's
office).
thaum souphanouvong kawm tiav bachelor of science (license) in civil
engineering from ecole national des ponts et chausees nyob paris
fabkis teb xyoo 1937 thiab los ua haujlwm nyob nam thanh, nyablaj teb
mas souphanouvong yuav pojniam nyablaj.  souphanouvong tus pojniam
nyablaj hu ua nyugen thi ky.
5.   huatais nplog tsevneeg (royal lao langxang hom khao luang
pranabg) puas tseem muaj neeg nyob tim nplog?
-muaj.  tabsi cov paub tab thiab cov tseemceeb tuag tag thiab khiav
tawm tuaj tag lawm.  cov mus tuag rau sam neua muaj:  king sisavang
vathana, queen khamphoui, crown prince tiao fasay vongsavang, tiao
tao, tiao bouavone, tiao khamsouk, tiao souphantharancy, tiao ker,
brig. gen. tiao vannaseng sayasan thiab coob tug colonels, majors
thiab cov nom yau.
huabtai tus kwv uas khiav tau tuaj rau australia yog brig. gen. tiao
sayavong mr 1 commander.  gen. tiao sayavong twb raug xa mus samana
nyob sam neua  (camp 5 at san tai).  xyoo 1986 gen. tiao sayavong mam
tawm los vientiane thiab khiav tuaj rau austrialia.
6.   koj nug hais tias history nplog  kuv kawm qhov twg?
kuv yog ib tug neeg ua liaj ua teb, ua tub rog, tsis kawm ntawv, tsis
muaj degree thiab kuv yeej tsis paub dab tsi.  tag amelicas cov
college thiab university yeej tsis muaj ib lub yuav qhia specific txog
history nplog, or tsov rog nplog thaum pib txog thaum xaus.
kuv teb tsis tau koj los thov zam txim.
