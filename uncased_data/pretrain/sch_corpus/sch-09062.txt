keep in mind, that for hmong, certain things are intended for only
specific things.  vim li no, only certain things are performed for
certain rituals.  a few years ago, there was someone asking about
"khawv koob"....specifically zaj khawv koob "yug tsi tau menyuam" es
ua zaj khawv koob kom "poob" los.  zaj khawv koob no siv tau rau
human babies xwb.  muaj ib tus xwbfwb, hlub hlub nws tus mes-es...tus
mes-es ntawd yug tsi tau nws tug menyuam, tus xwbfwb no muab siv once
rau tus mes-es ntawd....it worked...temsis after qhov no ces...zaj
khawv koob cia li tsi leej lawm.
another case, muaj ib zaj khawv koob ua rau tus poj niam (say due to
mistruss issues)...zaj khawv koob no, yog tias tus txiv tsi nyob tsev
lawm...mas ua kom yog tias tus pojniam tsi tsim txiaj ces 1.  ua kom
tus hluas nraug tsi muaj zog 2.  or yog tus hluas nraug muaj zog...es
yog
tias tau ua lawm....kom tus hluas nraug daig kiag... temsis, the qhov
"rule" mas tus xwbfwb yuav tsum qhia rau tus tub  hlob before he can
teach anyone else.  plus, zaj khawv koob no tsi yog ua rau kom tus hluas
nraug tuag....or pojniam tuag...ua kom nkawd tsi muaj zog or daig es
kom tebchaws ntes tau xwb...
anyway...back to qeej nruag.  qeej nruag muaj ntau ntau yam.
1.  qeej plig...tshuav anytime..anywhere los tau...but primarily for
tso plig xwb.  temsis there is no taboo in playing it antytime and
anywhere
2.  qeej tuag (txiag).  cov no los hais txog noob nrws, pluag, xub lawm
ces...tshuab anytime..anywhere los tau...there is nothing angainst it.
temiss hais txog cov qeej "kab ke" uas tuag (xws li tu siav, nce neeg,
hlawv ntawv...which involves muab tus tuag xa mus ceeb tsheej lawm ces
tsi "zoo" tshuav hauv tsev....koj yuav tshuab ces tawm nraum zoo los
yeej tau.  but again, cov qeej no ces are specifically made to be played
at funerals...temsis there is no "rule" to forbid playing elsewhere.
3.  qeej noj tsiab are made to be played during noj tsiab
4. qeej tshoob....are played at weddings..such as "qeej xa nyab", ua
"qeej/nkauj tawm tshoob"
5.  nyuj dab...made to play during nyuj dab event...temsis can be
play anytime..anywhere..
6.  tsa hauv toj....these are the fun ones...dhia qeej and tshuab qeej
during tsa hauv toj....around tus ncej ntxheb.
7.  qeej yug nruag......cov no muaj tsawg heeev...cov tibneeg uas yug
lub nruag..muaj ib zaj qeej txi lub nruag thiab....tshuab specificaly
right after lub nruag is made...es yuav coj los dai.
even though these types of qeej have there own specific time to be
played...you can pretty much play them anytime and anywhere li.  just
that hmoob tsi tshuav cov qeej tuag (kab ke xa tub tuag) hauv tsev
xwb....
hais txog lub nruag....twb muaj nruag neeb, nruag qeej.  hmoob tias "xob
ntaus nruag vim no one died...or sick.  hmoob tsi xav kom ua tej no vim
twb tsi muaj "use" for it at that time.
temsis, txog kev sis haum xeeb tsi yog los tej no los.  tej no involves
"cultural/religious" events lawm.  koj hais txog kev "social harmony"
which los ntawm tibneeg tus cwjpwm los lawm.
