ceebleej lis thiab toojzoo lis  nyob fresno xwb los mas yawg.  2 tug
no tsis yog hmoob vaj.  nkawv yog koj 2 nyuag kwv tij kiag ntag los
mas.  kuv kuj paub nkawv xov tooj thiab, tab sis kuv tsis yog nkawv
kwv tij, yog kuv muab nkawv xov tooj qhia tawm hauv no tsam kuv lam
raug plaub thiab.  nkawv twb yog koj cov kwv tij, koj yeej paub kawg.

tus kheej kam tsij zoj zeej mus investigate xwb ntag mas yus thiaj yog
tus neeg xav pab hmoob ov.  yog yus tseem tos txog thaum luag muab
proof los nplawv yus qhov muag tso ces thaum ntawv ces twb lig dhau
hwv lawm os.
