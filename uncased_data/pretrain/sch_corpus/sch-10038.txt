1. hmoob vaaj yuav tsu ua thawj coj xwb tsi pub lwm xeem ua hlo le 2. yuav tsum siv tuab tug ntawv xwb kws yog tug peb txiv huabtais vaaj pov pum zoo lossis cov vaaj pum zoo 3. yuav tsum koom tuab tug kaasmoos xwb kws yog tug peb txiv huabtais vaaj pov tsaa.

yog txhua yaam kws tsi nkaag rua peb nqe nuav cais yeej coj tsi tau hmoob lug sib koom ib zag.
txawm lwm xeem hmoob koom taag lawm los peb cov vaaj luj, vaaj miv nuav yeej tsi koom hlo le.

peb cov vaaj yuav tau paub qhov nuav zoo zoo nawb.
tug vaaj twg tseem moog koom lwm qhov cais tug ntawm tsi yog vaaj tabsis yog vaaj tug yeeb ncuab.

hmoobthawj zoo lawm laud zag nuav tes peb yawm huabtais hais le hov tes peb cuv vaaj yuav tsum siskoom es peb nrug peb tej muam ua tau neej lawm los?

thov qha hab vim yog peb moog yuav lwm xeem tes peb yuav tau koom nrug puab hab, kuv pum yawm px puab muaj txhais ntxim qab hab hov.
kuv hais le nuav vim kuv yug vaajntsab.

tsis txhob dlaag dlaag mas yawg hmobthawj.
lawv le kuv pum hab nov lug mas peb yawm txiv huabtais vp yeej ib txwm has tas lub xeem mas cia ua tshoob xwb hos hmoob ces yog ib tsev xwb no sav.
koj tsis paub tas txiv huabtais vp txawm yog tus kom hmoob ib xeem tsis txhob sis yuav lawm es kom ib xeem txhaj le hlub hab pab tau lwm xeem no lov?

luag cov hlub tej tswv yim kom hmoob caj haiv mas zoo tag tag le tawmsim mej cov miv nyuag yau tsis paub quav tsw ces txhaj le has le ub le nuav xwb sav.
