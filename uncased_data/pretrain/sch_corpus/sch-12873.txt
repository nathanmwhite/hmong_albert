"ua mob quas ntxhas tuaj nruab sab...ua noo yuj ywv tuj nruab plawv..."
this is a popular mong phrase regarding death; and i often wonder how it would be like once that moment arrives.  do you reflect on this child-like panic: that we all will face it at the end of 120 years? death is unbelievably disappointing--especially when the phrase "yuj ywv" comes into play.
most elders die just after their relatives come to visit and then leaves.  puas yog puab moog lawm tes peb moog hab?  ua caag peb tuag es peb tsi has ib lu dlaabtsi le--tuag tuab ywv xwb???
thaum kws koj nrug nwg thaam, ntawm npoo txaaj, koj has lug ntxhis rau nwg lub pobntseg, temsis koj tsi paub tseeb tas sov nwg nov los tsi nov.  los sov nwg nov koj has dlaabtsi.  yeej tsi xaav kuas nwg tuag le, but....from the outside, whatever comfort you try to whisper, you are still saying that this death is necessary--has to happen. tub tuag yeej tsi xaav tuag, temsis nwg tuag los tuag tuab ywv xwb, tsi has ib lu lug.  tug txheeb tug tshua yeej tsi xaav kuas nwg tuag, temsis xij has rua nwg tej qhov ntsej zoo le nwg tub tuag lawm.
puas yog vim muaj tej kev zoo le nuav es tej laug txhaj rov lug yuav nqe (nyuj dlaab)?  puas yog thaum tuag hov es tej lug has tsi taag?  ua caag tsi has thaum ua neej es tub tuag lawm, maam le lug has?  ua caag tsi yuav nqe thaum ua neej, es tuag lawm maam le lug yuav nqe?
i once heard of a story, where a father was dying (not literally on the bed).  the son also knew that the father was dying....so the son asked the father what he wanted before his death.  the father said, "tub, kuv yuav yuav ib tus nyuj dab ntawm koj.  koj them kuv tag, ces kuv mus kuv yeej tsi rov los taij li lawm os.  yog txiv neeb txiv yaig tseem tias yog kuv los taij nyuj dab thiab ces, yog lawd dag koj xwb..."  the son eventually tua ib tus nyuj rau nws txiv ua nyuj dab muab cob rua leej txiv lawm.  a few months afterward, the father passed away.
ua cas peb ua neej nyob, peb ho tsi coj li no?  ua mob los ua tibywv.  tuag los tseem tuag "tibywv" puas tsim nyog tuag na?
