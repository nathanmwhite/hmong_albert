age than you and me because they can have these services and they will
be fully paid for.  on the other hand, you and i will have the exhaust

already know i can't afford to live in america when i'm retired not to

being a retirement community for american old folks.  in the is hmong
retirement community, we can put in place all the service options such
as the above list so we can truly have choice and enjoy our
retirement.
