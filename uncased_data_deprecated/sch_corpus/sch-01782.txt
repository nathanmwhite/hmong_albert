the supreme court.
visitors to the capitol at washington on any week day from october to
june can see a group of nine distinguished-looking men, robed in silk
gowns, passing at noon through the long corridor into a room where a
clerk begins to call out, "oyez! oyez!" this is the supreme court of
the united states. it is the highest court in the land and the most
powerful tribunal in the would. it consists of a chief justice and
eight associate justices, all of them appointed for life by the
president with the consent of the senate. they cannot be removed from
office except by impeachment. their salaries cannot be diminished.
they are the guardians of the national constitution.
