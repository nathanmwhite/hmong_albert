report
rebecca sommer's written report of 139 pages, on the situation of the
hmong lao from the conflict areas, and the hmong refugees -- who
recently fled the conflict areas to thailand- was successfully
submitted  at special meetings, at the caucuses, to relevant un
agencies, governments, ipo's and ngo's, during the 5th session of
the permanent forum on indigenous issues.
-the report has been prepared in partnership with various united
states-based hmong organizations and individuals(such as the hmong
completed.they remain the most qualified experts on the subject.  for
these reasons, the report must be viewed as the product of a
collaborative effort.

report
rebecca sommer's written report of 139 pages, on the situation of the
hmong lao from the conflict areas, and the hmong refugees -- who
recently fled the conflict areas to thailand- was successfully
submitted  at special meetings, at the caucuses, to relevant un
agencies, governments, ipo's and ngo's, during the 5th session of
the permanent forum on indigenous issues.
-the report has been prepared in partnership with various united
states-based hmong organizations and individuals(such as the hmong
completed.they remain the most qualified experts on the subject.  for
these reasons, the report must be viewed as the product of a
collaborative effort.

report
rebecca sommer's written report of 139 pages, on the situation of the
hmong lao from the conflict areas, and the hmong refugees -- who
recently fled the conflict areas to thailand- was successfully
submitted  at special meetings, at the caucuses, to relevant un
agencies, governments, ipo's and ngo's, during the 5th session of
the permanent forum on indigenous issues.
-the report has been prepared in partnership with various united
states-based hmong organizations and individuals(such as the hmong
completed.they remain the most qualified experts on the subject.  for
these reasons, the report must be viewed as the product of a
collaborative effort.
