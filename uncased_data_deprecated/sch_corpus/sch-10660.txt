tiam (can these be appropriately labeled as hmong days of the week?):
1. hnub hmos ub/u (3 days ago)
2. hnub hmos (2 days ago)
3. nag hmo (yesterday/last night)
4. hnub no (today)
5. tag kis (tomorrow)
6. nag/naag kis (day after tomorrow)
7. puag nraus (3 days from now)
8. puag nraus u/ub (4 days from now)
dua li ces hmoob phaj hauj thiab hmoob catholic kuj tsim tau ib co npe
siv rau xya hnub ntawm ib lub lim tiam twg, thiab. tom qab nej xa nej
li tuaj tag kuv mam muab ob hom ntawv tso rau peb tau xyaum kawm,
thiab.
